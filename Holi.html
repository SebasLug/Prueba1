Holi

Holi is a popular ancient Hindu festival, also known as the "Festival of Colors" or the "Festival of Love". It is celebrated predominantly in India and Nepal, but has also spread to other parts of the world. The festival signifies the victory of good over evil, the arrival of spring, and the end of winter. During Holi, people gather to throw colored powders and water at each other, dance, sing, and enjoy festive foods. It is a time for forgiveness, renewal of relationships, and spreading joy and happiness.
